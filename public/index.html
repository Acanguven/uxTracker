FAQ's

How does UX Analytics work?
UX Analytics captures every mouse move, click, scroll and keystroke that a visitor makes inside a webpage, and then sends this information back to the UX Analytics servers in a highly compressed package. Our servers take a snapshot of the webpage as experienced by the visitor, and combine it with the visitor actions to recreate the original browsing session.
How it works
UX Analytics runs in the background of apps and capture screen and user interaction data. This data is visualized to help you better understand how your users interact with the app and lets you make informed decisions to eliminate customer struggle and improve usability.
How do I set up UX Analytics?
Price to UX Analytics and integrate our SDK. You can also invite your developer to integrate the code for you. The process itself takes less than 5 minutes.
Can I change plans or cancel?
Absolutely. You can upgrade/downgrade your plan or cancel your subscription at anytime you want from your account page in the dashboard.
Does UX Analytics provide aggregated data about links and visitors?
Yes, UX Analytics provides several reports based on aggregated visitor data. These reports include our Heatmaps, Link Analytics, Form Analytics etc. among others.
How secure is my data?
Very secure. UX Analytics takes several steps to ensure your data’s security. We restrict our employees’ access to your data. Our employees cannot access your data unless you provide us with your password and specific permission to access your account. HTTPS page data is passed to our servers via SSL and is fully encrypted. We regularly apply updates to servers, OS, firewalls and all our software to prevent security vulnerabilities.
Will UX Analytics slow down my site?
No. UX Analytics has no noticeable effect on webpage load times or site performance. The UX Analytics code is loaded at the end of the page, and is highly compressed. It therefore does not affect page load times at all.
How is UX Analytics different from traditional web analytics?
  • User expreriences (UX)

Why isn’t my question listed here?

Will the UX Analytics tracking code interfere with other JavaScript code?
No, UX Analytics will not interfere with any other JavaScript code. UX Analytics has been used successfully in conjunction with many other analytics packages including: Google Analytics, Omniture, JQuery, Prototype, etc.
